# Pixel to Inch Conversion
```
  1mm <=>   2px <=> 1/16"
  3mm <~>   4px <~> 1/9"
  3mm <=>   4px <=> 1/8"
  3mm <~>   4px <~> 1/7"
  6mm <=>   8px <=> 1/4"
  8mm <~>  10px <~> 1/3"
 12mm <=>  16px <=> 1/2"
 22mm <=>  28px <=> 7/8"
 25mm <=>  32px <=> 1"
 31mm <=>  38px <=> 1 3/16"
 32mm <=>  40px <=> 1 1/4"
 50mm <=>  64px <=> 2"
 56mm <=>  72px <=> 2 1/4"
 62mm <=>  80px <=> 2 1/2"
 75mm <=>  96px <=> 3"
 87mm <=> 112px <=> 3 1/2"
 ```
